So many ways to find jobs and make connections !

TECH-CONNECT




Tech-Connect is our annual, major-specific technology career fair held every spring, featuring approximately 40 local and national employers seeking to fill available positions within their organization. Tech-Connect is an opportunity for you to establish your presence in the technology world, network with potential future employers and learn more about the opportunities available within your specialized field.

BUILD A RESUME


Your resume is the first impression you will make on an employer, so it is very important for you to develop a professional, error-free resume. Career Services offers many online resources to assist you in composing a professional resume.
You can email your resume to resumereview@southeastern.edu to obtain a more detailed resume review, or you can contact our office at (985) 549-2121 to schedule an appointment with a Career Counselor.

FIND A JOB




LionHire is the new system that allows you to take care of your career preparation and job search needs - all in one place! In LionHire, you will need to create a free user profile. Once you've done that, you will be able to create a resume in the system, or upload a current one, apply for jobs and internships, sign up for On-Campus Interviews, practice interviewing skills, and take career assessments.
